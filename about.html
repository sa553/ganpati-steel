About Ganpati Steel

Ganpati Steel is a fabrication company based in Gurgaon, Haryana, specializing in modern aluminium, glass, and steel solutions for residential and commercial spaces. With over 20 years of experience in fabrication work, we are committed to delivering high-quality workmanship, precision installation, and durable structures.

Our expertise includes slim aluminium partitions, telescopic doors, folding doors, glass and aluminium railings, steel gates, and custom fabrication work. Every project is executed with careful planning, accurate measurements, and premium materials to ensure long-lasting performance and elegant finishing.

At Ganpati Steel, we believe that modern spaces require both strength and design. Our goal is to provide solutions that combine functionality, durability, and modern aesthetics while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship and timely project delivery.
